## Cross-Interview Findings

1. The magic number is 2+\* — all three respondents said the tool becomes
   useful once you have multiple subscriptions. Nobody with one sub feels pain.

2. Personal spend vs. company spend — none of the three had company-paid
   subscriptions. The real ROI for Credex is teams paying per-seat, not
   individual developers.

3. Nobody has audited their AI spend — not because they don't care, but
   because the complexity hasn't hit them yet. The tool needs to create urgency
   for people who don't feel the problem yet.

4. I can calculate it myself" — recurring objection from technically
   literate users. The value prop needs to emphasise team complexity and
   negotiated discounts, not just arithmetic.
